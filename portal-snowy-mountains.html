Overview

As a part of the Immersive Media Studio, we have developed this concept showcasing the ability for users to summon a portal utilizing augmented reality, facilitating their teleportation to a snowy mountain environment. Our initiative includes the organization of a Mixed Reality workshop aimed at acquainting participants with the realm of Extended Reality, while imparting the skills necessary to create similar portals using Bezi.
